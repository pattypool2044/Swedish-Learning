Lesson 22: First, Second - Första, Andra

We have been through the normal counting already in lesson 2. But how do you say 1st, 2nd or tell on which number of bus to take, or how many rooms an apartment has. Let me show you.

If someone ask how many room you have you can answer as following. If you have been looking around for a place to stay, you might have seen they wrote this as R.O.K or ROK. This seems to be only used for bed and living rooms, kitchen and bathroom is not included in that. Let me show you some sample questions and the answer below.

Question Eng Fråga Swe Svar Swe Answer Eng Audio
How big an apartment does Perry have? Hur stor lägenhet har Perry? (1 r.o.k) Etta One
How big an apartment does Lisa have? Hur stor lägenhet har Lisa? (2 r.o.k) En tvåa Two
How big an apartment does Jerry have? Hur stor lägenhet har Jerry? (3 r.o.k) En trea Three
How big an apartment does Mandy have? Hur stor lägenhet har Mandy? (4 r.o.k) En fyra Four
How big an apartment does Dean have? Hur stor lägenhet har Dean? (5 r.o.k) En femma Five
How big an apartment does Melanie have? Hur stor lägenhet har Melanie? (6 r.o.k) En sexa Six

If some ask or you ask which bus to take to a certain place they say the numbers a bit diffent. See below some samples

Question Eng Fråga Swe Svar Swe Answer Eng Audio
Which bus goes to the airport? Vilken buss går till flygplatsen? (30) Trettian Thirty
Which bus goes to Tivoli park? Vilken buss går till Tivoli-parken? (12) Tolvan Twelve
Which bus goes to the cinema? Vilken buss går till biografen? (43) Fyrtiotrean Forty three
Which bus goes to the library? Vilken buss går till biblioteket? (11) Elvan Eleven
Which bus goes to the hospital? Vilken buss går till sjukhuset? (107) Hundra sjuan One hundred seven

One last example with numbers is if you are still going to school, people can ask you in which grade you are. See below how to answer this.

Question Eng Fråga Swe Svar Swe Answer Eng Audio
In which grade/year does Maja go? I vilken årskurs går Maja? (åk. 1) I ettan 1st
In which grade/year does Bastian go? I vilken årskurs går Bastian? (åk. 2) I tvåan 2nd
In which grade/year does Jessica go? I vilken årskurs går Jessica? (åk. 3) I trean 3rd
In which grade/year does Sam go? I vilken årskurs går Sam? (åk. 4) I fyran 4th
In which grade/year does Lady go? I vilken årskurs går Lady? (åk. 5) I femman 5th
In which grade/year does Max go? I vilken årskurs går Max? (åk. 6) I sexan 6th
In which grade/year does Savannah go? I vilken årskurs går Savannah? (åk. 7) I sjuan 7th
